Welcome to Cat’s Meow Café

We offer a comfortable and relaxing atmosphere where you can enjoy food, coffee, movies and cats. In partnership with The Humane Society of Harrisburg, Cat's Meow Café houses eight rescue and shelter cats who are ready to interact with you in the hopes of being adopted. In addition to our furry stars, we have an impressive café menu that's perfect for lunch breaks and meetups with friends.


Our ultimate goal is to create a fun environment where patrons can get involved with finding these beautiful cats their forever homes. Since our opening in 2015, we have seen 20 of our Café cats adopted. We encourage you to come and get to know us! Every month the café is running fun events for our dedicated patrons, so make some plans to come visit us soon!
